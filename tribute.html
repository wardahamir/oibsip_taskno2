Biography
Steven paul "Steves" was an American information
technology enterpreneur and inventor. He was the
cofounder, chairman, and CEO of Apple Inc, CEO and
largest shareholder of Pixar Animation Studios; and
founder, Chairman,and CEO of NeXT Inc. Jobs is widely
recognized as a pioneer of the microcomputer revolution
of the 1970s,along with Apple co-founder Steve Wozniak
